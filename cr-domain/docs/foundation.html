Everything specializes eight universal categories (the Category-Level Objects), all rooted in a single top:Core. A small shared top keeps the model legible; the richness lives in the domain leaves.

Layers add specificity: top (universal) → hcls (health & life sciences) → cr (clinical research). Each flow page below specializes these.

Universal DNA

Every entity, whatever its category, carries three strands: identity (top:identifier), time (top:observedAt — the canonical NGSI-LD valid-time term), and lifecycle (top:status). top:UniversalDNAShape enforces it. This is what makes any two TOP graphs line up by construction.

The non-negotiable spine

  • Bitemporal: every assertion carries valid time (true-in-world) and transaction time (system knowledge); the past is reconstructable and back-dating is impossible.
  • Provenance: W3C PROV on every fact — who, when, on what evidence.
  • PII containment: identifiable hcls:Person is boundary-only; downstream attaches to the pseudonymous cr:StudySubject; the only legal bridge is the attested Enrollment.
