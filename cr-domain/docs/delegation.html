ruleapplies toseverityrequires

  • Delegation must grant a Capability.
  • Delegation must name the delegate.
  • Delegation must name the responsible delegator (PI).

  • Act exercises a capability with no delegation of that capability to its performer (undelegated work).

  • Act performed outside the effective window of its delegation (timing gap) — review materiality.
