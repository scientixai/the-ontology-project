Architectural patterns established

Study consolidates three architectural patterns established by earlier specs:

  1. Per-Org-per-Study Sponsor (Sponsor v0.1.4): a Study is reached by multiple Sponsor entities representing different jurisdictions, financial vs operational responsibility, or CRO-as-proxy delegation. Study itself doesn't carry sponsor responsibility flags; those live on Sponsor.
  2. Operational hierarchy with StudySite pivot (Site v0.2.0): Site → StudySite → Study → Protocol → SOA → Visit → Activity. Study is the pivot between operator-side and definition-side. StudySite is the per-Site-per-Study join entity.
  3. Sub-object containment for design entities (Study v0.3.0): Protocol, Arm, ScheduleOfAssessments, Endpoint, InclusionCriterion, ExclusionCriterion are sub-objects of Study; their lifecycle is bound to the Study. Future top-levels with similar contained-design semantics (e.g., Visit's sub-objects) follow the same pattern.
